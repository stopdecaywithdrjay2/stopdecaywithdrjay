Hi, let me introduce myself. I am Dr. Jay Ascher and welcome to my website. I am a family dentist in private practice and have been at this location since 1975. I am a Fellow of the Academy of General Dentistry - a very prestigious accomplishment. I currently teach in the General Practice residency program at Long Island Jewish Medical Center and I am part of the faculty practice at North Shore University Hospital. My fellowship and faculty positions show my commitment to continuing education and keeping up with what is most current in dentistry. Please enjoy your visit.

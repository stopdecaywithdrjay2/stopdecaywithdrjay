My office is dedicated to the highest quality of care. Only the finest materials are used in the construction of our prosthetics and all are ADA certified and approved. We treat patients with respect to their time and finances and offer an array of convenient office hours and financial options.

We accomplish our treatment in the fewest possible appointments and employ both local anesthesia and Nitrous Oxide (sweet air). There is always an open dialogue so that we can answer all of your questions before, during and after treatment. Your satisfaction is our goal.

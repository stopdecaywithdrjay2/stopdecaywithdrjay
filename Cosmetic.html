Bonding restores damaged teeth that have been chipped, cracked, misaligned, or discolored. A plastic resin is used to rebuild the teeth and make the look natural. They can be made to look whiter and straighter without the need to see and orthodontist.
Bleaching- we use a very efficient take home product in a custom tray made exclusively for you. Office dispensed products are more effective than whiteners bought at retail outlets. The otc products have a much lower concentration of bleach and some use materials or trays that can damage your teeth and gums.

Our office uses safe, effective materials to create your white smile.
Porcelain Veeners - These are tooth shaped shells that are adhered(bonded) to your prepared teeth. They can correct discolored, damaged, or cooked teeth. They can change the color and shape for a more natural smile, while closing spaces.

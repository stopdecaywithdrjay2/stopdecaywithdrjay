General dentists can treat early stages of gum disease.

Gum disease is an inflammation or infection of the gums and bone that support the teeth. It's caused by bacteria in plaque.

Studies show a strong link between gum disease and heart disease, strokes and low birthweight babies.
Symptoms:
  • Bleeding during brushing/flossing
  • Changes in your bite
  • Shifted or loosened teeth
  • Red, puffy gums
  • Pus between teeth
  • Gum tenderness and pain
  • Persistent bad breath
We will customize a plan to treat your problem.
